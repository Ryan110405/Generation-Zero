The prototype classes

The prototype class of machines are orange and don’t have a lot of armor.They are the Lowest tier of machines
and the ones you’ll come across first.


The military classes

The military classes have different shades of green mixed in with the camo.
They have a lot more armor than the prototype classes.


The FNIX classes

The FNIX classes have a lot more armor than the military classes but have more very advanced weapons.
FNIX Machines are mostly black with red wiring.


The apocalypse classes

The apocalypse machines have the same amount of armor as the FNIX machines, but they have a lot of different
weapons. They either have incendiary rounds or uranium laced bullets, mine launches, artillery, flamethrowers and
radiated rockets.


The Reaper

The Reaper is a Tank that the machines built; It's your rival! The Reaper is a boss fight for the players.
The Reaper is armed with an incendiary heavy machine gun, radiated rockets, a shield to protect itself from incoming damage,
shockwave, and a thermobaric explosion.


Soviet ranks

Soldier class

the soldier class is the prototype of the soviet classes and are the weakest; Their coloring is tan.


Scout class

The Scout class is stronger than the soldier class, but weaker than the Spetsnaz class. The machines are all green.


Spetsnaz class

The Spetsnaz class is covered in white winter camo and has two more weapons to ruin your day. This class is the
highest machine class from the soviets
