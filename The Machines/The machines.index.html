The Tick

The Tick is found in bunkers and can be dropped by some machines. The Tick can jump and do some damage to you, but
you can easily one shot it.


The Seeker

The Seeker is a drone like machine that when spotted will make a siren and alert all the machines in the area to
run to that position.


The Dog

The Dog or Runner is a four legged machine that can have a sub machine gun, shotgun, or rocket launcher. Designed
to be as mobile as possible. The easiest way to kill the Runners are to shoot the top canster on top of them.


The Hunter

The Hunter is a humanoid machine that will give you a fright when you see it. It walks on two legs and can be
in a pack of three to six. They can be armed with a heavy machine gun, A chainsaw, sniper rifle, gas rockets, and
sticky shotgun explosive. To easily take these machines out, you must shoot just to the right of it and
shoot the canister on the back.


The Harvester

The Harvester is a huge four legged machine that walks around and harvests materials.
The harvester is still deadly though, It has two rocket pods and a shockwave.
Not only does it have defense it has an offensive side. The harvester can call in
hunters for support. To kill a harvester you can blow up the three gas canisters on the back of it,
destroy the rocket pods,
and hit the front components.


The Tank

The tank walks on two legs and has lots of weapons to make your day suck. It can be armed with a dual heavy machine gun,
Rocket pods, mortars, and a modified sniper. These are its offensive weaponry, but you can shoot of the weapons and get
rid of them. Be prepaired when you do that though, the tank will full on run at you and try to trample you.
Theres a gas canister on the back of them which if destroyed does a lot of damage.
